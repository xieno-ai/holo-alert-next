# Holo Alert — Brand Writing Brief

A reference document for anyone writing copy for Holo Alert.

---

## Who We Are

Holo Alert (Holo Technologies Inc.) is a **proudly Canadian company** headquartered in Edmonton, Alberta. We were founded with a single mission: to help Canadian seniors live safely and independently — on their own terms.

We are not a faceless American tech company. We're Canadian, our support is Canadian, and we understand the needs of Canadian seniors and their families.

---

## What We Do

Holo Alert provides **medical alert systems** — wearable devices that connect seniors to a live 24/7 monitoring centre at the press of a button (or automatically in the event of a fall). If something goes wrong, trained monitoring professionals respond immediately and dispatch help — whether that's a family member, neighbour, or emergency services.

Our devices work **anywhere in Canada** — no Wi-Fi, no smartphone, no complicated setup required.

## Our Purpose

Falls are the leading cause of serious injury among Canadian seniors. Many seniors live alone or spend significant time without family nearby. The fear of falling — or of not being found in time — causes real anxiety for both seniors and their adult children.

Holo Alert exists to solve that problem. We give seniors the confidence to keep living life fully, knowing that if something goes wrong, help is always one button away. And we give families peace of mind knowing their loved one is protected, even when they can't be there in person.

**We are not a panic button. We are an independence enabler.**

## Our Audience

### Primary: Canadian Seniors (Ages 55–80)
- Living independently at home
- May have experienced a fall or health scare
- Value their independence deeply
- Not necessarily "tech savvy" — simplicity matters
- Don't want to be a burden to their family

### Secondary: Adult Caregivers (Ages 40–65)
- Adult children of aging parents
- Often living in a different city or province
- Worried about a parent living alone
- Looking for a solution that gives them peace of mind without taking away their parent's independence

## Brand Voice & Tone

**We are:** Warm, empathetic, trustworthy, clear, empowering

**We are not:** Fear-mongering, clinical, patronizing, condescending

Our brand never makes seniors feel helpless, frail, or like a problem to be managed. We celebrate independence. Aging should empower, not limit.

**Always:**
- Speak to the reader directly ("you")
- Lead with a benefit or relatable pain point
- Emphasize independence, confidence, and freedom
- Position monitoring as a safety net that enables living fully — not a sign of decline

**Never:**
- Use scare tactics or graphic imagery of accidents/falls
- Make seniors feel old, weak, or dependent
- Use generic, clinical, or cold language
- Overpromise or make unverified medical claims
